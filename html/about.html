ABOUT

Alkaline LLC is both a clothing label and a board company. Founded in 2019 by Matt Le Cras, the brand aims to fuel from and be conductive to skateboarding culture. The brand draws creative inspiration from the massive popularity of skateboarding in the 90’s as well as motorcycles, nature, art from the 60’s to present, and the electricity and chemistry associated with the brand’s name.

Matt Le Cras is the founder and CEO of Alkaline LLC. He is a devoted skater and is very passionate about art and design. Matt is also a mechanical engineering student at the Ohio State University.

Matt currently produces all of the art and graphics for Alkaline LLC, but is open to bringing on more people to help with this and some other miscellaneous needs of the company in the future. If you are interested with working with us, equity investing, or are just interested in more company info, you can find contact information for Matt under our ”Contact Us” page.
